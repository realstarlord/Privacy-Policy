DISBET Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to our Discord Betting Bot. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our services. By using our bot, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Discord Username: We need your Discord username to identify you while using the bot.
  • Account Data: We collect data about your betting activity, such as your current balance, bets placed, and outcomes.
  • Technical Data: We may collect non-personally identifiable information related to your usage of the bot, such as your Discord client version and interaction logs.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide our services: Your information is used to manage your bets, track winnings, and provide you with an interactive betting experience.
  • To improve our services: We use collected data to enhance bot performance, fix bugs, and offer new features.
  • For security: We use your data to monitor for fraudulent or abusive activity and ensure a safe environment for all users.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services to you, and as required by applicable laws. If you wish to delete your data, please contact us directly.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion. However, no data transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data and request information on how it is being used.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request the deletion of your data, subject to applicable laws.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies to collect personal information about you.

Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. Any changes will be communicated through our Discord bot, and a revised version will be posted on this site. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the data we collect, please feel free to reach out to us via our Discord support channel or email us at contact.disbet@proton.me.
